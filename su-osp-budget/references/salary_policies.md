# New York State Salary Thresholds and University Policies

## NYS Exempt Employee Salary Thresholds

New York State requires minimum salaries for FLSA exempt employees (professional, executive, administrative).

### Current Thresholds

**Rest of New York State (including Syracuse):**
- **2025:** $60,405.80 annually ($1,161.65/week)
- **2026:** $62,353.20 annually ($1,199.10/week)

**NYC, Long Island, Westchester:**
- **2025:** $64,350 annually ($1,237.50/week)
- **2026:** $66,300 annually ($1,275/week)

### Affected Positions at Syracuse University

**Must meet minimum exempt salary:**
- Postdoctoral Scholars (Account 513001)
- Research Associates (Account 513002)
- Senior Research Associates (Account 513005)
- Exempt Staff (Account 513021)
- Other professional exempt positions

**Not affected (no salary minimum):**
- Faculty (professional exemption, no state threshold)
- Graduate Students (not exempt employees)
- Hourly/Non-exempt employees

### Compliance Checking

When calculating postdoc or research associate costs:
1. Check if proposed salary meets current NYS threshold
2. If below threshold, flag compliance issue
3. Recalculate with minimum compliant salary
4. Show both proposed (non-compliant) and minimum costs

**For searches:** Query "new york state exempt salary threshold [current_year]" if threshold data is >6 months old.

## Course Buy-Out Policy

### Standard Course Buy-Out

**Cost per course:**
- **15% of academic-year base salary + fringe + F&A** (if on grant)

**Limits:**
- Maximum **2 courses per academic year** without Provost approval
- Must have funding available at start of semester
- Same fiscal year as funding source

**Approval required:**
- Department chair/school director AND dean
- For sponsored projects: OSP approval **before proposal submission**

**Funding sources:**
- Sponsored projects (grants/contracts)
- Internal funds (startup, research subsidy, etc.)
- Gift funds
- Cannot self-fund with personal fellowship money

### Full Teaching Buy-Out

**Cost:**
- **80% of academic-year base salary + fringe + F&A** (if on grant)

**Requirements:**
- **Provost approval required**
- Granted only under extraordinary circumstances
- Service obligations remain (not bought out)
- Teaching obligations fully eliminated

### Special Case: NIH Salary Cap

For faculty with salaries above NIH cap (~$225,700):
- Course buy-out based on **9-month equivalent of federal salary cap**
- NOT based on actual salary
- Example: If AY salary is $300,000, use capped amount for buy-out calculation

### Course Buy-Out Examples

**One course from NSF grant (AY salary $100,000):**
```
Salary (15%): $15,000
Fringe (29.5%): $4,425
MTDC: $19,425
F&A (49.5%): $9,615
Total: $29,040
```

**Two courses from startup (AY salary $100,000):**
```
Salary (30%): $30,000
Fringe (37.5%): $11,250
F&A: $0 (no F&A on internal funds)
Total: $41,250
```

## Graduate Student Stipends and Policies

### Minimum Stipend Levels (Union Rates)

**PhD Students (Full-time, 20 hrs/week, 9 months):**
- 2024-2025: $28,000 ($35.90/hr)
- 2025-2026: $28,750 ($36.86/hr)
- 2026-2027: $29,500 ($37.82/hr)
- 2027-2028: $30,000 ($38.46/hr)

**Master's Students (Full-time, 20 hrs/week, 9 months):**
- 2024-2025: $24,000 ($30.77/hr)
- 2025-2026: $24,250 ($31.09/hr)
- 2026-2027: $24,750 ($31.73/hr)
- 2027-2028: $25,000 ($32.05/hr)

### Payment Structure

**Academic Year Appointments:**
- **18 semi-monthly payments**
- Appointment period: August 16 - May 15 (9 months)
- Paid on 15th and last business day of month

**Summer Appointments:**
- Period: May 16 - August 15
- Hourly rate equals academic year rate
- ≥10 hours/week requires appointment letter

**Mandatory Annual Increases:**
- Students AT or ABOVE minimum get raises:
  - 2024-2025: 4.0%
  - 2025-2026: 3.0%
  - 2026-2027: 2.75%
  - 2027-2028: 2.75%

**For Grant Budgeting:**
- Use **3% annual escalation** for future years
- Unless sponsor prohibits inflationary increases

### Graduate Student Benefits

**Paid Time Off:**
- Vacation: 4 days per semester (20-hour appointment)
- Sick time: 56 hours per calendar year
- Parental leave: Up to 8 weeks

**Union Coverage:**
- Syracuse Graduate Employees United (SGEU), SEIU Local 200United
- Covers TAs, RAs, Admin Assistants
- Does NOT cover hourly employees

## Faculty Appointment Structure

### Tenure-Track and Tenured Faculty

**Academic Year Appointments:**
- **8.5 months** (standard)
- Monthly rate = AY salary ÷ 8.5
- Account: 500011 (Faculty)

**Summer Salary:**
- Charged separately at monthly rate
- Account: 530011 (Faculty - Summer)
- Different fringe rate (15.8% vs. 29.5% for AY)

### Research Faculty

**Account:** 500013 (Research Faculty FT)

**Appointment Types:**
1. **12-month appointments** (most common)
   - Calendar year
   - Paid semi-monthly
   - Same fringe rate year-round

2. **8.5-month appointments** (some cases)
   - Academic year structure
   - **No separate summer rate** (unlike tenure-track)
   - Uses Account 500013 for all periods

**Salary Source:**
- Typically from grants/research funds
- Can also be paid from institutional funds (startup, etc.)
- "Derives exclusively from research funds" is aspirational, not absolute

## Salary Escalation

**Standard Practice:**
- **3% annual escalation** for calculating future year costs
- Applied to all personnel categories
- Unless sponsor specifically prohibits

**Example:**
- Year 1: $50,000
- Year 2: $51,500 (×1.03)
- Year 3: $53,045 (×1.03²)
